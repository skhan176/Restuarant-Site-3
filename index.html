Oasis Cafe

The restaurant covers the whole ground floor and is organizaed in two dining rooms: The dining rooom Oasis Dine, with capacity for 30 guests with a cosy fireplace And the dining room Oasis PALOMA conceived for small celebrations or business lunches. This last dining room can sit up to 80 guests. It is equipped with an over head projector and a screen, very useful for some events. This restaurant was inaugurated in 2004 and offers traditional cooking with a touch of modernity. Local quality products are mainly used in this kitchen (meats, salt preserved meats, octopus, vegetables, fish form the nearby Ría de Vigo…) to be combined with new tastes and textures.
